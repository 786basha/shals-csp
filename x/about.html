"Andhra Pradesh is renowned for its hand-woven clothes since ancient times. The weavers of the state have expertise in producing the most exclusive hand woven sarees most of which have a gorgeous look, and they even produce dress-materials that have delicate and distinctive designs. Exclusive to Andhra Pradesh, Ikat technique of weaving is extremely popular throughout India.Dharmavaram which is a small town in the Anantapur District is well known for its rich handloom works / sarees. The silk sarees of Dharmavaram have earned world wide fame and these sarees have broad borders with solid colors that mostly contrast the body color. Padmashali.
